Vegetable Lasagna

Description

This Creamy Vegetable Lasagna is loaded with fresh summer veggies, three types of cheese, and the most delicious cream sauce! A delicious alternative to traditional lasagnaâ€¦.and perfect for summer!

Ingredients

  • 2-3 cups mixed vegetables (Broccoli, Carrots, Onions, etc.)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 4 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1 package lasagna noodles
  • 1 can Ragu Alfredo sauce
  • 1 tablespoon italian seasoning
  • salt and pepper to taste
  1. Boil the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. While noodles are cooking. Saute veggies in skillet.
  2. When veggies are tender add seasonings, alfredo sauce and simmer for 10 min.
  3. In 9x13 baking dish, layer lasagna noodles, sauce and cheese until it is 3/4 full. Make sure cheese is top layer.
  4. Bake covered for 35 min. Take foil off and then bake for another 5 min to brown the cheese.
